Bored Ape Culture Club is a Season 0 project of the NftyDreams DAO. Its goal is to use the community and reach of the Bored Apes brand to promote art and culture through Web3.

Ape holders challenge artists to create interesting derivatives of their ape and award DAO tokens as prizes to winning artists. The derivatives are then sold to the public making the Bored Ape brand more accessible. Holders form tribes within the DAO and vote on interesting art and culture projects funded by the DAO.

The Bored Ape Culture Club adds tremendous value to the BAYC brand and aligns incentives through the DAO token for apes interested in art and culture.
